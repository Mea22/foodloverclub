Khinkali

Khinkali dumplings originated in the mountainous regions of Georgia where the colder climate called for more substantial, nourishing food, and they have been enjoyed for centuries.

Ingredients:

For Meat fillings

  1. 2 small onions (4oz/115g/each), peeled and minced
  2. 1.3 pound/600g ground lamb or a combination of pork and beef
  3. 1 teaspoon hot red pepper flakes, more to taste
  4. 1 teaspoon ground caraway seeds
  5. 1 tbsp red sumac
  6. 1 1/2 teaspoons kosher salt
  7. 1 2/3 cup/400ml boiled water, at room temperature
  8. 1/2 cup/20g finely chopped cilantro (optional)

For the dough:

  1. 3 3/4 cups/500g unbleached all-purpose flour, plus more for kneading and rolling
  2. 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  3. 1 cup/240ml warm water
  4. Freshly ground black pepper for serving
